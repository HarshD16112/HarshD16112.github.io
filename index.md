  <section class="section">
    <div class="section-label">Research Focus</div>
    <h2>What I work on</h2>
    <div class="card-grid card-grid-2">

      <div class="research-card">
        <h3>Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH)</h3>
        <p>Developing Pt-based multimetallic catalysts (Pt–Sn, Pt–Ga, Pt–Fe, Pt–Cu) for on-purpose propylene production. Integrating XAS, Raman, and CO pulse chemisorption with ML-driven descriptor engineering.</p>
        <span class="research-tag">XAS / ME-XAS</span>
        <span class="research-tag">Pt catalysis</span>
        <span class="research-tag">Operando</span>
      </div>

      <div class="research-card">
        <h3>Machine Learning for Catalysis</h3>
        <p>Building interpretable ML workflows using Random Forest, LightGBM, XGBoost, SHAP, and Bayesian Optimization to screen combinatorial catalyst spaces and identify high-performing formulations.</p>
        <span class="research-tag">Bayesian Opt</span>
        <span class="research-tag">SHAP</span>
        <span class="research-tag">LightGBM</span>
      </div>

      <div class="research-card">
        <h3>Oxidative Dehydrogenation (ODH)</h3>
        <p>Vanadium-based (V–Mo–Zr–Ti/Al₂O₃) catalyst systems for ODH of light alkanes. Systematic characterization via CO-TPD, H₂-TPD, CO₂-TPD, XPS, and TGA-MS.</p>
        <span class="research-tag">V-based catalysts</span>
        <span class="research-tag">TPD / TPR</span>
        <span class="research-tag">Kinetics</span>
      </div>

      <div class="research-card">
        <h3>CO₂ Hydrogenation to Methanol</h3>
        <p>Published review (CCR, 2023) on Cu- and non-Cu-based catalytic systems for CO₂ → MeOH. Emphasis on structure–activity relationships and reaction mechanism analysis.</p>
        <span class="research-tag">CO₂ utilization</span>
        <span class="research-tag">Review article</span>
        <span class="research-tag">CCR 2023</span>
      </div>
